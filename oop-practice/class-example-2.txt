Playground Class
//Attributes
isOpen (bool)
size (float)
name (string)
equipment (arr)
//Methods
close(sets is open to false)
expand(increases size by 500 sq ft.)
rename (changes the name of the playground to the new string)
addEquipment(adds new piece of equipment like "monkey-bars" to the equipment array)
destroyEquipment(removes piece of equipment like "swings" from the equipment array)
weather(reduces the size of the playground)
